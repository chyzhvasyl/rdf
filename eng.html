about company

FDI is a group of IT companies from Ukraine which develop analytical systems and business applications. Created by practicing professionals from banking sector in 2011 as a system integrator for automating activities of financial institutions, the company has gained a considerable experience in creating software and developed its own data array management platform.

History ⟩

FDI team has already implemented a number of complex management and database systems and continues to work actively to create data storages and analytical and reporting systems for customers from private and public sectors. Since 2016, our specialists have been working on automation and electronic document flow projects for Kyiv city government.

Products ⟩

Skillful work with data arrays and analytics is our advantage. Using modern technologies such as Java and NodeJS, we are able to introduce the most innovative ideas.

products

WHITEANT

WHITEANT analytical system is designed to set up and form managerial statements for companies of financial sector. The system also integrates with banking software through API.

City advertising space management

The automated system is designed to keep record of and manage city advertising objects. The system includes open API for data exchange between different city departments and users – operators of an advertisement.

City municipal property registration

The program makes it possible to keep a detailed record of and manage municipal property. Thanks to open API, there is data exchange between city departments, owners and applicants, and necessary reports and documents are generated.

“Maino” Information Analytical System

The complex is designed to organize and service the city property register and display it on a map using the created geographic information system. The map may contain an unlimited number of information layers and generate reports on the basis of a sample of information on property.

Demographic register

The registering system keeps record of changes in the birth rate and death rate, helps generate documents requested by citizens and has a separate API for integrating with other products.
